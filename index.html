You can also use the "strong" element to bold text within a line.

You can also italic select words.

You may run into the "b" and "i" tags. While these tags will bold and italic your text, they have no meaning to the browser. This is particularly important when considering website accesiblity. The browser will not emphasize the "b" and "i" tags like it will "strong" and "em"
